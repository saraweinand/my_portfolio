    Visual Communication Design Survival Kit

    As a student, we are continuously learning about how to navigate school and life simultaneously, occasionally we need a little guide to help us through. I created this kit as kind of a joke, but after the final product, I hope I can make this survival kit a reality for incoming design students.

    The Content

    The survival guide is the meat and bones of the kit. Inside the guide, I included things like the Gestalt Principles, Typography rules, Principles of design, how to guides, and advice from Eastern's VCD Alumni. All the things I wish I knew when I became a design student.

VCD Survival Kit

I came up with this idea when i found my self drowning in a project and I jokingly said to myself, "I need a Design Survival Kit!" I decided to run with the idea and incorprated all of the things I have found myself reliant on. ie. Exacto knives, moleskines, extra flashdrives, energy(lots of energy), and of course... chill pills. As for the survival guide, I included many of the ideas and principles that would have come in handy in my early days of the design program.

If you would like to see more than pictured, let me know! This project was a blast and I'd love to share more of it!
